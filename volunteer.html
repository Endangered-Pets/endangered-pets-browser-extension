ABOUT US

CREATION

As a part of our programming project in college. Four students from Trinity College Dublin were commissioned by Software Engineers, Panpan Lin and Peter Poliwoda at IBM to create a chrome browser extension and a informative website to raise awarness for criticallly endangered species, namely the Pangolin.

BROWSER EXTENSION

We created a Chrome Browser Extension available in the chrome web store that allowed users to interact with their very own virtual pangolin pet. They can take care of their pet and are encouraged to keep a low number of tabs open or their pet will become sick and die.

WEBSITE

We developed a dynamic, fun and infomative website so that people can find out more information about criticallly endangered species.

THE FUTURE

It is our hope through the creation of an open source git repository that devolopers will add further fucntionality and endangered pets to the project!
